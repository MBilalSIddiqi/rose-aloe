What has been added

- Multipage storefront structure with dedicated pages for home, shop, about, contact, and FAQ.
- Dedicated product pages for individual soaps, serums, shampoos, face washes, and gift sets.
- Shared product data setup for consistent rendering across listing pages and detail pages.
- Improved homepage hero with a cinematic brand banner and stronger visual hierarchy.
- Smarter shop search with ranked matching, aliases, and live search suggestions.
- Product cards refined with cleaner actions, tags, prices, and links to detail pages.
- Contact page redesigned with a branded hero, contact cards, message form, and FAQ preview section.
- FAQ page added with grouped accordion sections and a call-to-action support panel.
- Product detail visuals split by product type:
  - soaps show as bars
  - serums use slimmer bottles
  - shampoos and face washes use bottle-only visuals
  - gift sets use a wrapped gift-box visual
- Cart persists across pages with localStorage.

- Add a mobile menu for smaller screens.

- Add product image galleries and thumbnails on detail pages.

- Add highlighted matching text inside live search suggestions.
