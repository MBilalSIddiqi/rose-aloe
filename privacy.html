Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

Introduction

Rose Aloe values the trust customers place in the brand. This policy outlines how personal information is collected, used, stored, and protected when someone visits the website or places an order.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, phone number, email address, and delivery details.
  • Order details: products purchased, order notes, and gifting requests.
  • Technical details: browser type, approximate device information, and pages visited.
  • Communication details: messages sent through the contact form or WhatsApp support.

How We Use Information

  • To prepare, confirm, and deliver customer orders.
  • To answer support questions, gifting requests, or stockist inquiries.
  • To improve the browsing and shopping experience on the website.
  • To follow up about an order, if clarification is needed.
  • To protect the site from misuse or suspicious activity.

Information Sharing

Customer information is not sold or rented. It may be shared only when necessary with trusted service providers, delivery partners, or when required by law.

Cookies and Site Data

The website may use basic browser storage or similar tools to support cart behavior, improve usability, and understand how visitors move through the site.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to keep customer information safe. No online system is completely risk-free, but care is taken to limit access and use information responsibly.

Your Choices

  • You may request updates to incorrect personal information.
  • You may ask questions about what order-related information is being used.
  • You may request removal of information where legally and operationally possible.

Third-Party Services

Some customer communication may happen through third-party services such as WhatsApp. Those platforms maintain their own privacy practices and policies.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the date above will be refreshed.
