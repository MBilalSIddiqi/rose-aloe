Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

Overview

We take pride in every bar, bottle, and gift box we send out. If you are not satisfied with your order in the cases described below, we will work with you on a refund or replacement.

Eligibility for a Refund

  • Product arrived damaged during shipping.
  • Product is materially different from what was described on the website.
  • Product has a clear manufacturing defect.
  • The wrong item was sent due to an error on our side.

Not Eligible

  • Products that have been opened, used, or partially used.
  • Products damaged after delivery due to storage or handling.
  • Reactions related to known personal allergies to listed ingredients.
  • Change of mind on scent, color, or finish.
  • Requests submitted more than 7 calendar days after delivery.

Return Window

You have 7 calendar days from the day the order is delivered to request a refund or replacement. After this window, we are unable to process a return.

How to Request a Refund

Message us on WhatsApp at +92 334 3601488 or email info@pureessencesoaps.com with the following:

  • Order number or order date.
  • Reason for the return.
  • Photos of the damaged or defective product, if relevant.
  • Preferred resolution: refund or replacement.

Processing Time

Once we receive your request and confirm eligibility, refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days using the original payment method where possible. Replacements are dispatched on the next available shipping run.

Shipping Costs

If a return is approved due to our error or shipping damage, we cover the return shipping. In other cases, return shipping is the customer's responsibility.

Contact

  • WhatsApp: +92 334 3601488
  • Response time: Within 24 hours on business days.
